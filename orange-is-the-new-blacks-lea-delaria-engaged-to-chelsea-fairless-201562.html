Orange Is the New Black's Lea DeLaria Engaged to Chelsea Fairless

Big Boo is getting hitched! Orange Is the New Black’s Lea DeLaria is engaged to her fashion editor girlfriend Chelsea Fairless. The actress announced the happy news via Facebook on Friday, Feb. 5.

“Yes bitches I am engaged,” the 56-year-old wrote. “Despite their concerns about the patriarchal confines of marriage, actor / singer Lea DeLaria & editrix Chelsea Fairless of Brooklyn, New York are pleased to announce their engagement,” she continued. “No date has been set for the impending nuptials but it’s safe to say that it will be the event of the season, or a total s–t show.”
