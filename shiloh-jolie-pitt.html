Shiloh Jolie-Pitt News - Us Weekly

Name:Shiloh Jolie-Pitt

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Relationship:Single

Birthday:May 28, 2006

About

The first biological child of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, Shiloh Nouvel Jolie-Pitt has become a Hollywood icon. Born on May 28, 2006 in Namibia, Africa, the public was captivated by the blonde beauty from the start, although her mother was less intrigued.

In a January 2007 interview with Elle U.K., the actress said: “A newborn really is this…Yes, a blob!” However, over the past few years, it is clear that Shiloh has become a vital part of the Brangelina clan.

Since her birth, Shiloh has become a baby who bops all around the globe: she has already jetted to India, New Orleans, Cambodia and most recently Cannes, France. Shiloh doesn’t have to leave her house to find playmates; she has five siblings: Maddox, Pax, Zahara, and twins Knox and Vivienne.
