Jamie Foxx's Daughter Annalise Meets Taylor Swift, Jay Z at Grammys

What a lucky girl! She may not have been up for any awards, but Jamie Foxx’s 5-year-old daughter Annalise quite possibly had the most memorable night ever at the 57th Annual Grammy Awards at L.A.’s Staples Center on Sunday, Feb. 8. The Annie actor made sure to introduce her to many of his mega-famous friends, including Taylor Swift, Madonna, and Jay Z.

Foxx, 47, posted a few of the special moments on Instagram during the star-studded event. “Jay and my little one,” he captioned a sweet pic of Annalise with Beyonce’s hubby. Cute as a button, the young fan held up two peace signs for the black-and-white snapshot.

Annalise then moved on to Katy Perry, who wrapped her in her arms. “Thanks @katyperry for making my daughter’s night! #Grammys,” Foxx captioned. Pharrell Williams also got in on the fun. Foxx wrote: “And now she’s HAPPY. @pharrell.”

The Oscar winner was eager to jump in for some pics too. Annalise showed off her adorable dimples while posing in separate photos with Swift, Madonna, and Rihanna, who held her up with Foxx while wearing her pink Giambattista Valli ball gown. Annalise met Brits Ed Sheeran and Paul McCartney as well.

Foxx is also dad to daughter Corinne, 20, whom he shares with another ex. He’s kept tight-lipped about the mothers’ identities for years.
