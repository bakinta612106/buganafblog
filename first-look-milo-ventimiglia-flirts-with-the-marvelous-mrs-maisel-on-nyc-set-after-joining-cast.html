FIRST LOOK: Milo Ventimiglia flirts with the Marvelous Mrs. Maisel on NYC set after joining cast

This Is Us star Milo Ventimiglia was spotted on the Manhattan set of The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel on Thursday for the first time since joining the cast of the Amazon hit series’ fourth season.

The Anaheim-born 43-year-old and the Wisconsin-born 30-year-old appeared to have great chemistry as they giggled and flirted in a series of scenes they shot at Central Park.

And while Milo’s character has yet to be revealed, he’s clearly important to Rachel’s stand-up comedian Miriam ‘Midge’ Maisel.
