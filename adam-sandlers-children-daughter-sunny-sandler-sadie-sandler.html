Meet Adam Sandlers Children; His Daughter Sunny &amp; Sadie Sandler

Adam Sandler has done it all! He was hugely successful in Standup comedy, and at one point was the world’s richest actor with a movie grossing over $2 billion.

He is a massive success in big screens, and a great husband to one of the world’s most successful models Jacqueline Samantha Titone, AKA Jackie Sandler. With his beautiful wife, he is a father of two beautiful children.

Jackie Sandler & Adam Sandler Marriage

The 50 first Dates actor, is a happily married man and his story with Jacqueline Samantha Titone started in a set of a movie. At the time, Jackie was a successful model but was trying her hands in acting. While playing in a minor role in Big Daddy as a waitress, Jackie first met her future husband, Adam. The movie released in 1999 and fans shortly found out that they were dating since then.

After that, she appeared in several Sandler movies which proved that the pair was inseparable. Jackie even converted to Judaism for Adam in 2002, who is one of the funniest jew men in Hollywood. After she showed a serious commitment towards Adam by changing her religion, the couple got engaged the same year.

Finally after a year of their engagement, on June 22, 2003, the duo tied the holy knot in Malibu at Dick Clark’s oceanfront state. Their wedding was absolutely star-struck as it was held in the presence of actors like Rob Schneider, Jennifer Aniston, Rodney Dangerfield, and some of his co-star friends.

Though many of his big-Hollywood friends could have best maned the ceremony, his bulldog, Meatball, dressed like Adam in a black tux became his best man.

Now, the happy couple is the proud parents of two beautiful daughters Sunny Sandler and Sadie Sandler. Their eldest, Sunny recently stepped into her teens whereas the youngest one is in her late childhood.

Sadie Sandler

On 8th October, the father and his two daughters were seen on-stage covering A Taylor Swift song where Adam was seen playing the Guitar, and his daughters were handling the Mic business.

Surprisingly, at a tender age, both his daughters have started acting. So Let’s know Adam’s daughters in their short bio here!

Sadie Sandler Biography

Sadie Madison Titone-Sandler is basically a celebrity child of a superstar actor father Adam Sandler and mother Jackie Sandler who is a supermodel. Still, this aspiring actress has already graced the big screens alongside her actor father, Adam.

She first appeared in Adam’s movie Grown Ups in  2010, then she appeared in Jack and Jill in 2011, Hotel Transylvania in 2012, and finally in Pixels released in 2015.

Sadie Sandler is the first baby daughter of Adam and Jackie Titone born on May 6, 2006, in Los Angeles CA. She is currently thirteen years old teenager studying in her seventh grade.

Sunny Madeline Sandler

Sunny is Adam and Jackie’s second daughter and to be honest, and she has more of Adam’s personalities in her. She is more joyful on TV screens, and the little kid is goofier, which means her parents have a correct name that defines her character.

She also has acted in Jack And Jill, Grown Ups, And Pixels alongside most of her family members.

Sunny was born on 2nd November 2008 in LA, California. Sunny grandparents are Judy Sandler, Stanley Sandler, Lila Titone, and Joseph Titone. She has had numerous small roles in movies, but this little girl made a lasting impression on the viewer’s hearts after she appeared in a film Blended released in 2014.
