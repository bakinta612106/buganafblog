James Charles Car Collection: 2023 Cars of James Charles - 21Motoring

James Charles Dickinson is a popular American beauty YouTuber and makeup artist. He started his YouTube channel in 2015. His YouTube channel has more than 24 million subscribers. And in 2016, he became the first male brand ambassador for Covergirl. James also has his makeup line in collaboration with Morphe Cosmetics. He is a recipient of two People’s Choice Awards, three Streamy Awards, one Shorty Award, and one Teen Choice Award. Charles has an estimated net worth of $22 Million. Let’s take a look inside his luxurious car collection.

Contents

1. Rolls-Royce Ghost 

It has a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that makes 563 hp and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost can go from 0-60 mph in 4.3 seconds and has a top speed of 155 mph, and costs $312,000. This automobile is among the most incredible cars in the James Charles Car Collection.

2. Tesla Model X

Next comes the Tesla Model X in the Youtuber James Charles Car Collection. The Model X comes with dual motors that make 503 hp and 487 lb-ft of torque. It can go from 0-60 mph in 3.0 seconds with a top speed of 130 mph. Tesla’s flagship electric SUV costs $103,000.

3. McLaren 12C

It is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that makes 616 hp and 443 lb-ft of torque and can go from 0-60 mph in 3.2 seconds and has a top speed of 205 mph. The McLaren costs $240,000.
