Kansas City Chiefs Tyreek Hill engaged to Keeta Vaccaro

There are serious fireworks between these two.

Kansas City Chiefs star Tyreek Hill got engaged to girlfriend Keeta Vaccaro on the Fourth of July.

The 27-year-old wide receiver posted a series of photos to his Instagram of the moment he got down on one knee in front of a sign that said “Will You Marry Me” in big letters at a Fourth of July party.

“The beginning of forever ❤️,’ he captioned the post on Tuesday.

When she said “yes,” fireworks exploded in the background and the newly engaged couple shared a kiss.

Vaccaro, 25, also posted a video to Instagram of the joyous moment they agreed to share their future.

“My forever ♾??,” Vaccaro, who is the sister of NFL star Kenny Vaccaro, captioned the video.

She also showed off her massive engagement ring, a square diamond with a diamond halo and a pavé band.

“She’s definitely got the most diamonds, man,” Hill told TMZ of the ring. “We made sure of that, you know what I’m saying?”

He said the proposal came as a complete surprise to Vaccaro.

“It was perfect, you know?” he told TMZ. “I had her thinking the whole time like she was planning the whole party and she was in contact with the event planner and I was going behind her back and talking to him like, ‘Hey, we’re going to do this and that. Just go with it.’”

Hill and Vaccaro haven’t set a wedding date yet, but he told TMZ that he’ll make sure he and Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes — who is engaged to Brittany Matthews — don’t overlap.
