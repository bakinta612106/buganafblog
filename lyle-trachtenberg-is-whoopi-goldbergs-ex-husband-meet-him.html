What Does Whoopi Goldberg’s Ex-husband Do for a Living?

Lyle Trachtenberg has already graduated from college before he went to pitch his tent with IATSE (International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees). IATSE is the recognized body that monitors the activities of the entertainment industry. It functions as the labor union in the world of show biz. The body is charged with the responsibility of serving technicians, crafts persons, and artisans in theater, TV, trade shows, and movies.

We don’t know exactly when Lyle joined IATSE, but by the mid-1990s, he had already attained the status of a popular influential organizer. The IATSE activist gathered notoriety from the body’s troublemaking actions on the sets of independent films. Lyle was akin to a producer’s nightmare as his behavior made several of them lose too much funds.

Lyle Trachtenberg was particularly harsh on the likes of indie moviemaker Allison Anders in 1995 while she was producing her movie, Grace of My Heart. Alison was barely into the second week of production when Lyle Trachtenberg descended on her with his trouble-making colleagues. It happened that the female moviemaker had non-union crew members in her team when Lyle came in at lunchtime. Denied entry into the catering truck, the IATSE personality’s spirit was not doused one bit as he stood outside listing all the benefits to be derived from joining IATSE.

Talks became rather extremely intense later and was spiraling into the discussion of embarking on a makeshift strike. However, before lunch was over, the body gained additional 25 members. Alisson and her crew were eventually given the go-ahead order to continue filming following an amicable agreement between unionists and producers.

Alison’s case was just one out of the deluge of Lyle’s trouble-making activities. The influential organizer’s unpalatable behavior has become the nightmare of movie producers as several of them came out to narrate their experiences.

Trachtenberg’s bad reputation in IATSE notwithstanding, he has remained one of its active members to date.
