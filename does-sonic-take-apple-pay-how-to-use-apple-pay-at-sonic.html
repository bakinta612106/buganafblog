Does Sonic Drive-In Take Apple Pay?

Yes, Sonic supports Apple Pay, and they declared that the payment system will be available on November 2, 2019. For customers who pay using Apple Pay, Sonic is renowned for offering discounts and special promotions. The ability to make quick and easy purchases is Apple Pay’s major advantage for the drive-in restaurant company.

How Can I Pay With Apple Pay at a Sonic Drive-In?

Using Apple Pay at Sonic is a reasonably simple process.

  • Bring all the items you want to buy to the checkout counter.
  • To verify your identity, open your Apple iPhone and use Face ID or Touch ID.
  • At the checkout counter, choose the card you want to use.
  • Each Sonic will feature a contactless reader that will automatically scan items for purchases.

Can I use Apple Pay at Sonic and get cashback?

If you use the Apple Pay Card and the merchants accept it, Apple will give you cashbacks. The cashback has no transactional restrictions, so you can use it as often as you like. Every transaction is eligible for a cashback bonus of up to 3%. Visit the Apple website to review the precise terms and conditions.

Customers of the Apple Pay Card often receive rewards for any purchases made with the card. On the official Apple website, you can determine your eligibility. It is advised to use the Apple Pay Card if you are a frequent shopper so that you can receive cash back on each purchase. Over time, these savings add up to be enormous.

Using Apple Pay at Sonic Drive-In has numerous advantages

Sonic and Apple have been collaborating to make it simple for customers to do simple transactions.
