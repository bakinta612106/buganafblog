Jena Malone Gives Birth to First Child

Jena Malone just revealed that she has given birth to her first child, and it’s a beautiful baby boy!

The actress announced on Instagram, that she and her boyfriend, Ethan DeLorenzo, have welcomed their first-born son.

Malone wrote, “The best weekend of our lives!!!,” on Instagram. “What an incredible blessing to be chosen by this amazing , kind , gentle and beautiful soul to be his parents. Humbled and in complete awe that we get to experience the most ancient and transcendent love that exists.”

The happy couple also revealed that their son’s name is Ode Mountain DeLorenzo Malone – how cute!
