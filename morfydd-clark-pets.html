Morfydd Clark

Birthday

March 17, 1989 (35)

Morfydd Clark is a Welsh actress. She has played a few notable roles including Maud in Saint Maud, Dora Spenlow in The Personal History of David Copperfield, Mina Harker in Dracula, and Galadriel in The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power.

In December 2019, Morfydd was announced to play a younger Galadriel in the Amazon Prime Lord of the Rings prequel, The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power. The series debuted on the streaming service in 2022 and is considered one of the most expensive television series ever produced. The series estimated a cost of $715 million which worked out to $89 million per episode. The series attracted over 25 million viewers on the first day.
