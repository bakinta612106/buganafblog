Jon Gosselin Says Kate Gosselin Is Not In Contact With Hannah And Collin Reveals When He Spoke T

It’s no secret that Jon Gosselin has been estranged from six out of his eight children for a long time as he’s mentioned that before. Now, however, the reality TV star got into more detail about the last time he spoke with Cara, Mady, Leah, Alexis, Joel and Aaden and also revealed whether or not his ex wife, Kate Gosselin ever talks to Collin and Hannah, the two kids that live under his roof.

Jon was on Dr. Oz and in a sneak peek of the interview, he makes it very clear that the six not only no longer talk to him but have stopped communicating with their siblings, Hannah and Collin, as well!

The latter live with him and his current girlfriend, Colleen Conrad.

As for when it was the last time he spoke to his estranged young ones, Jon Gosselin mentioned that it’s been no less than two years and blamed Kate for their relationship breaking.

The man tells Dr. Oz that ‘parent alienation and now sibling alienation’ are what led to their estrangement.

‘And now they’re shunning Hannah and Colin,’ he went on to mention, stressing that Kate is actively responsible for the siblings’ lack of communication, without mentioning what her motivation for that would be.

In addition, he claimed that Kate no longer speaks to her two children that live with him either and, in fact, she never even contacted them to let them know about selling their childhood home.

As fans know, the Gosselins purchased the Pennsylvania farmhouse way back in 2008, when the sextuplets were only 4.

Instead, Jon learned about the house being listed for sale through word of mouth.
