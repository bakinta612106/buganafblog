NBA Draft 2023

The 2023 NBA Draft, also referred to as the 77th edition, is set to occur on June 22, 2023, at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York.

Unlike previous drafts that featured 60 picks, this year’s event will have 58 picks.

The reduction in the number of picks is a direct consequence of the Chicago Bulls and the Philadelphia 76ers having their second-round selections revoked due to violating the NBA’s tampering regulations during the free agency period.

It’s noteworthy that this marks the second consecutive season in which the NBA draft will consist of 58 picks instead of the customary 60, once again attributable to tampering violations.
