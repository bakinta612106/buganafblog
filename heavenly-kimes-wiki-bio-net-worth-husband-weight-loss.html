Career

Heavenly Kimes’ career is multi-dimensional. She is a multi-talented woman who is an excellent dentist, motivational speaker, author, businesswoman, and television personality.

Heavenly Kimes is most famous for her role in the American reality show Married to Medicine. She joined the main cast during the second season.

Heavenly Kimes from Married to Medicine show

Heavenly Kimes is the developer of SmileChangers Tooth Whitening. This is a product that helps whiten teeth without any sensitivity. She specializes in cosmetic dentistry, implants, veneers, Invisalign and every other branch of dentistry. She has been able to build her own Dental Empire in Atlanta by the name Smiles by Dr. Heavenly.

She has authored the book The Business of Love and co-authored Wake-Up. . . Live the Life You Love.
