And it starts with that connection we make with people and wildlife every day. Because when wildlife thrives, all life thrives.

How much money does it cost to go to the San Diego Zoo?

  • San Diego Zoo Admission Prices

Adults (ages 12 and up) cost $62, and children (ages 3-11) are $52. Kids under three are free. For an additional six-dollars per ticket, guests can also experience one of the 4-D theater experiences.

San Diego Zoo Safari Park Package Options

At the Zoo Safari Park, there are several safari packages guests can add onto their admission ticket.

  • $53 for the Cart Safari and Jungle Ropes Safari
  • $57 for the Cheetah Safari
  • $77 for the Flightline Safari
  • $87 for the Behind-the-Scenes Safari
  • $121 for the Caravan Safari
  • $145 for the Roar and Snore Overnight
  • $675 for the customized Ultimate Safari.

Again, safaris do not include admission prices.
