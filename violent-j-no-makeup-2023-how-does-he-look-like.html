Joseph Frank Bruce, stage name Violent J, born April 28, 1972, is a multifaceted American artist.

He’s renowned as a rapper, record producer, and professional wrestler, notably a part of the hip-hop duo Insane Clown Posse (ICP).

Together with Shaggy 2 Dope (Joseph Utsler), he co-founded Psychopathic Records and Juggalo Championship Wrestling.

Bruce’s creative ventures span music, wrestling, and entrepreneurship, leaving an indelible mark on both industries.

His contributions, from co-founding Psychopathic Records to his role in ICP, underscore his influence as a dynamic figure in entertainment, captivating audiences with his diverse talents and endeavours.

Violent J No Makeup Look 2023

In 2023, Violent J of the iconic rap duo Insane Clown Posse (ICP) showcases a distinct no-makeup appearance that contrasts his on-stage persona.

Born Joseph Francis Bruce in Berkley, Michigan, Violent J has evolved from his heavily painted clown aesthetic to a more natural look.

He was renowned for his co-founding role in the Psychopathic Records label and significantly influenced the music and underground culture scenes.

Without his trademark clown makeup, Violent J’s face reveals a unique individuality.

Sporting a small face tattoo on each cheek, he retains his distinctive flair while embracing a more subtle expression.

His features include a prominent forehead, short hair, and his moustache and beard, reflecting his transition towards a mature and refined appearance.

Beyond his musical endeavours, Violent J has demonstrated a deep commitment to fostering a sense of community among fans.

The adoption of face paint by ICP members, affectionately known as Juggalos, has transcended mere aesthetics.

It serves as a means of connection and identification, cultivating a shared sense of belonging within the ICP family.

Violent J’s journey from flamboyant clown makeup to his current no-makeup look symbolizes his growth as an artist and individual.

His decision to remove the heavily stylized appearance showcases a willingness to embrace authenticity and vulnerability.

As he continues to influence the music industry and subcultural landscape, Violent J’s evolving appearance mirrors his evolving artistic expression—one that resonates with the spirit of ICP and its dedicated following.

Violent J Health Update 2023

During this year’s Gathering of the Juggalos, the annual event for fans of Insane Clown Posse (ICP), a poignant announcement was made by the duo.

Violent J, whose real name is Joseph Bruce, revealed he has been grappling with heart failure.

Violent J’s physical appearance has undergone a significant transformation throughout the years due to his efforts to lose weight.

Severe knee problems exacerbated by obesity prompted the rapper to shed pounds.

While he didn’t disclose the precise cause of his heart condition, he candidly shared the extent of his struggle before a crowd of devoted Juggalos.

Describing his journey, Violent J recounted how he experienced breathlessness while walking to his studio, a mere 40 yards from his home.

Concerned, he sought medical attention and underwent an ultrasound, during which the medical professional’s demeanour hinted at alarming findings.

Subsequent consultations led to a diagnosis of heart failure, a revelation that deeply rattled him.

In his pursuit of recovery, Violent J underwent various treatments for Atrial Fibrillation, a condition where the heart’s upper chambers beat irregularly.

This condition often manifests through palpitations, shortness of breath, and weakness. Despite his medical endeavours, he continued to face challenges with his heartbeat.

Addressing the future, Violent J disclosed plans for a comprehensive farewell tour encompassing the United States, Europe, Canada, and Australia.

This decision doesn’t indicate a complete cessation of performances, as the duo intends to participate in monthly Juggalo-related events.

Notably, he assured fans that their creative output remains unaffected by his and Shaggy’s health conditions.

As he navigates his health journey, his dedication to music remains unwavering, ensuring that the impact of Insane Clown Posse continues to reverberate.
