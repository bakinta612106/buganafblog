The Game Season 7, Episode 4: Missed You, Kelly Pitts

“The Game” season 7, episode 4: “Missed You, Kelly Pitts” premieres tonight on BET.

In this week’s episode, Jason and Chardonnay throw Brittany a pre-prom party.  Meanwhile, Jason gets an unexpected surprise when his ex-wife, Kelly, shows up at his door. Also, Keira tries to get back with Blue.

“The Game’ is a half-hour comedy that takes a behind-the-scenes look at the dysfunctional football family of the San Diego Sabers, as they navigate fame, relationships and the pressures of professional football. Three women, each with a boyfriend, husband or son on the same mediocre football team, will stop at nothing to help their respective guys turn the team around, forming unexpected friendships in the process. The Game is executive produced by Mara Brock Akil, Salim Akil, Kenny Smith, Erica Montolfo-Buro and Kelsey Grammer.”

“The Game” season 7, episode 4:  “Missed You, Kelly Pitts” airs Tuesday, March 18, at 10 p.m., on BET. Check out a sneak peek below.
