Who is Steve Perry:

Steve Perry is a famous Rock Singer. He was born on January 22, 1949 and his birthplace is Hanford, CA. Steve is also well known as, The lead vocalist of Journey from 1978 to 1997, he released nine albums, including the 1996 platinum album Trial By Fire. He also released solo work, like the #3 song “Sherrie.” 

Steve is originated from United States. He was joined by guitarist Neal Schon in Journey.
