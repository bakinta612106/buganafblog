Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus chose to put a stop to their marriage and longtime relationship a year ago and it’s safe to say that both of them have moved on! However, one insider report claims to know that Liam’s life, in particular, is ‘completely different’ now!

The unnamed source tells E! News that Liam Hemsworth has totally put his failed relationship with Miley Cyrus behind him and that things are not what they used to be for him – in a good way!

They dished via the news outlet that ‘Liam is happy to have moved on and is living a completely different life now. The way that things ended between them didn’t sit well and he was saddened by it all. It took Liam time to finally come to terms with the end of the relationship and to process it. He knows that he’s in a better place and they needed to move on from one another.’

In other words, Liam Hemsworth’s broken heart has been mended in the meantime and his recent lunch date with Gabriella Brooks seems proof enough!

The couple was seen out and about, just enjoying some fresh air and each other’s company in Byron Bay just a couple of days ago and their romance seemed as strong as ever!

As fans know, Liam has actually been mostly in Australia ever since the split with Miley Cyrus and it sounds like being back home and with his family has helped a lot with the healing process as well.

The insider explained that the actor ‘needed to get out of town and just away from all to be able to gain some perspective.’
