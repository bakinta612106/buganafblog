Who is John Velazquez:

John Velazquez is a famous Horse Jockey. He was born on November 24, 1971 and his birthplace is Puerto Rico. John is also well known as, Puerto Rican horse jockey who amassed over 4,000 wins in his career, including a 2011 win at the Kentucky Derby.

John is originated from Puerto Rico. He and Willie Shoemaker became two of the winningest horse jockeys.
