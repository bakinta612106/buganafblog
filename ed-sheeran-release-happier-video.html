Ed Sheeran to release music video for 'Happier' next week

Award winning singer and songwriter Ed Sheeran has announced through his social media today that he will be releasing a new music video for his single ‘Happier’ next week.

The redheaded musician took to Instagram to share a short video which was promoting the highly anticipated music video. The single comes from his third album ‘Divide’ which has seen him receive success after success, with the album even going straight in at number one when it was released in March last year.

The teaser which he has released to his fans shows a puppet, representing himself holding up a banner with the words “video coming soon!”. Ed explained in the caption that the video release date is next week. His fans are going crazy and are extremely excited to see the video, as a lot of the popular songs from ‘Divide’ have gone without an official video.

Ed has begun touring the album already with it kicking off in Italy at the start of last month, playing at some of the biggest stadiums in the world. He is set to finish his tour at the beginning of November. The tour has completely sold out, even after him adding extra dates!

Keep your eyes peeled on Ed’s social media for the release of the video, we can’t wait to see it.
