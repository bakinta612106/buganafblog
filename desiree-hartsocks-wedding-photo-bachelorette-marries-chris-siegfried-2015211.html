Desiree Hartsock's Wedding Photo: Bachelorette Marries Chris Siegfried

And they lived happily ever after! Desiree Hartsock‘s Bachelorette love story got a storybook ending on Sunday, Jan. 18, when she said “I do” to fiance Chris Siegfried. The couple exchanged vows in a wedding in Palos Verdes, Calif. — and only the new issue of Us Weekly has all the exclusive details and pictures from their special day.

Hartsock, 28, who got engaged to Siegfried, 29, on the season nine finale of The Bachelorette in May 2013 (it aired three months later in August), gave Us an inside look at the nuptials, including photos of her two dresses, her bridesmaids, and her first dance with her new husband.

The six-page spread also features exclusive details on their vows and their guest list, which boasted several fellow Bachelor Nation stars.
