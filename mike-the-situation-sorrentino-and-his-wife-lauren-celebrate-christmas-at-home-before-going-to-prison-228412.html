Mike The Situation Sorrentino And His Wife Lauren Celebrate Christmas At Home Before Going To

As fans know, Mike ‘The Situation’ Sorrentino has been sentenced to prison and he is set to start his time in only a few weeks! That being said, the man made sure to celebrate his last Christmas at home and make it one to remember!

Mike and his life partner, Lauren Sorrentino look cheery in a pic featuring the two of them posing in front of their Christmas tree together.

The man captioned the sweet snap with: ‘Merry Christmas from The Situations and don’t forget to treat thyself.’

The Situation was sentenced to no less than eight months behind bars for tax evasion back on October 5.

Court documents claim he is expected to surrender to the Bureau of Prisons before January 15, meaning that he only has a couple more weeks to spend at home with his wife.

Lauren is very supportive, and actually, the two made sure to get married in an unforgettable ceremony after the sentence had already been determined, on November 1.

The ceremony took place at Legacy Castle in Pompton Plains, NJ.

One source described the wedding to E! News as very ‘emotional,’ adding that ‘everyone was thrilled to see them at such a happy, solid place. People were so happy for them. It was jubilant.’
