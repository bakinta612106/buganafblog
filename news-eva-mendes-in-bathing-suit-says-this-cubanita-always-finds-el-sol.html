Mendes shared some of her wellness tips in an interview with Shape. She says that she makes sure to eat healthy. Mendes also says that she makes sure to get enough protein, especially in the mornings for breakfast. "I mostly keep it simple and have scrambled eggs and a piece of Ezekiel toast," she said. "I find that when I start out with the right source of protein, it definitely helps."

When it comes to her diet, Mendes isn't about variety. She tells Shape that she mostly eats the same healthy options each day. "I'm a creature of habit that way," she said. "I don't get bored with food. I try to think about it as fuel for my body." Mendes cites salmon with rice or quinoa as a go-to for her. 

Mendes makes sure to workout. She shared with Shape that she likes to run, and that she does different types of running exercises. "I love doing intervals," Mendes says. "Like running and sprinting, and I see great results with that versus the 30 to 45 minutes of steady cardio I used to do on the treadmill." Running is a cardio, aerobic, and endurance exercise. 

Mendes eats healthy, however, she also isn't afraid of indulging. She tells Shape that one of the things that motivates her to stay healthy is her love of indulging. "Sweets are my thing," she reveals. "The kid in me thinks, I work out, so I can support my sugar habit. I know that's not a very wise thing to say, but I really do feel like I'm working it off!"
